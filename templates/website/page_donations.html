Thank You to Everyone Who Donated

PyCon Africa 2025 has concluded. Thank you to everyone who generously donated to support our community. Your contributions made a real difference in opening doors to the Python community across Africa.

What your support enabled

Your donations helped us:

Beginner's Day

We welcomed beginners to our FREE hands-on workshops, helping them discover new career paths, develop skills, and find a community where they belong.

Community members supported

We helped people who wouldn't otherwise have been able to attend:

  • African community members building local tech scenes
  • Students exploring what's possible
  • Teachers bringing programming back to classrooms
  • Young professionals growing their skills

What we built together

PyCon Africa is more than just a conference. We exist to open doors, connect people, and plant seeds that grow into careers, projects, and stronger communities. As a completely volunteer-run, non-profit event, all the money raised went directly toward making Python more accessible to people who might not otherwise have had the chance to get involved.

Your support helped us welcome more people into our community.

How we work

Since we're completely volunteer-run, donations went directly to participants and programming. Our organizers contributed their time because they believe in what we're building.

We don't have overhead costs or salaries eating into contributions. Donations funded opportunities, experiences, and futures.

Why this matters

Technology is growing rapidly across Africa, but learning opportunities aren't equally available everywhere. Your support for PyCon Africa helped remove barriers that prevent talented people from getting involved in tech.

You also helped build bridges between developers who might otherwise work in isolation, and created the kind of catalyst experiences that turn curiosity into careers and careers into community leadership.

The people we supported go on to support others in their own communities. The impact multiplies.
