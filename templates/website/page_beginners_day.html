What Is Beginner's Day?

Beginner's Day is a special pre-conference event dedicated to welcoming newcomers into the Python community. Through hands-on workshops and supportive mentorship, we're opening doors for aspiring developers, data scientists, and technologists from all backgrounds.

Our mission:

Create an inclusive, supportive environment where anyone can start their Python journey, regardless of their technical background or experience level.

Advanced Pythonistas Welcome Too!

We are all beginners at something. Even if you're an experienced Python developer, these workshops cover a wide range of topics that might be new to you - from data visualization with Plotnine to game development with PyGame.

Learn Something New

Expand your Python toolkit by exploring areas outside your expertise. Data scientists can try game development, web developers can dive into data visualization, and everyone can discover new libraries and approaches.

Share Your Knowledge

Join us as a coach! Your experience can help guide newcomers through their first Python steps. Teaching others often deepens your own understanding and gives back to the community that helped you grow.

Why Beginner's Day Matters

Technology thrives on diverse perspectives and fresh ideas. By making Python accessible to everyone, we're:

  • • Breaking down barriers that prevent people from entering tech
  • • Increasing diversity in the Python community and broader tech ecosystem
  • • Creating pathways for underrepresented groups to discover their potential
  • • Building confidence for those who might feel intimidated by technical conferences
  • • Fostering inclusion by providing a welcoming entry point to PyCon Africa

How to Attend

Step 1: Purchase Your Ticket

Purchase your Beginner's Day ticket for R50 (approximately $2.50) through our ticketing system.

Don't worry - this event is completely free! The ticket fee ensures your commitment to attend and helps us plan accurately. We will refund your full R50 payment when you check in on event day.

Important: If you purchase a ticket but don't attend the event, we cannot provide a refund. This policy helps us maintain our limited capacity for those who are truly committed to participating.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need any programming experience?

A: Not at all! Beginner's Day is designed for people with no prior programming experience.

Q: What if I'm not selected?

A: We'll maintain a waitlist and notify you if spots become available. We also encourage you to check out our online resources and join our community channels.

Q: Can I attend both Beginner's Day and the main conference?

A: Yes! You'll need separate tickets for the main conference, but we offer discounts for Beginner's Day participants.

Q: What operating system should my laptop have?

A: Windows, Mac, or Linux all work fine. We'll help with setup regardless of your system.
