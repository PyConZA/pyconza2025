Help us make PyCon Africa amazing!

PyCon Africa is a non-profit event, organized by the community, for the community. Everything we do is powered by passionate volunteers who believe in Python and open source.

Why Volunteer?

Volunteering is a fantastic way to connect with fellow Python enthusiasts from across the continent, make lasting friendships, and expand your network. You'll be directly contributing to the success of the conference and helping create an inclusive space where knowledge is shared and ideas flourish.

Connect

Meet amazing people from across the Python community

Give Back

Help create an inclusive and welcoming conference experience

Make an Impact

Be part of something that matters to the African tech community

What you can do

We have a lot of different roles available. You can see them documented in detail here.

None of the roles require specialized knowledge. We'll show you how to do everything.

Setup

Help to lay out our registration area, sponsors area and signage on the morning of 9 October

Teardown

Help to pack up all our equipment and signage at the end of 10 October

Session Audio/Visual

Monitor the AV equipment, steer the video camera, take care of the microphones. No expert knowledge is required

Session Chair

Introduce the speakers to the audience, handle Q&A, keep track of time

Remote Session Chair Assistant

Monitor the Discord channel related to a talk, help select good questions for the session chair to pass onto the speaker

Remote Help Desk and Vibe

Hang out on our Discord help desk channel and help anyone who asks, make people feel welcome and engaged by chatting on our other channels

Workshop Monitors

Introduce workshops to the audience and keep an eye on things so they run smoothly
